Key points (Optima Secure)
- Applicability: All waiting periods and exclusions apply individually for each Insured Person; claims are assessed per person.

  - Treatment of pre‑existing diseases and their direct complications is excluded until 36 months of continuous coverage from the first policy inception date, unless the Policy Schedule says otherwise.

  - If you have continuous prior coverage and port the policy per IRDAI portability norms, the waiting period is reduced to the extent of prior coverage.

- Reimbursement claims — time limits

- Cancellation
  - You (the Policyholder) may cancel the policy with 7 days’ written notice; the company will refund pro‑rata premium for the unexpired period.
- Exclusions (example)

- Conditions and discharge
  - Policy terms and conditions are precedent to the insurer’s liability.
  - Any payment to you, your nominees, legal representative or to the hospital will be a valid discharge of the company’s liability for that claim.
